5G NR is the new radio access technology developed by 3GPP for the 5G mobile network. It offers high speed, low latency, and supports a wide variety of use cases including enhanced mobile broadband, IoT, and ultra-reliable communications.

  • PDCP, RLC, MAC, PHY

Beamforming is a technique to direct signal in specific directions. Massive MIMO uses multiple antennas to enhance capacity and reliability in 5G NR.

5G NR supports scalable numerology: 15, 30, 60, 120, and 240 kHz subcarrier spacing to support diverse deployment scenarios and latency requirements.

Key Lessons from Ikigai

1. Discovering Your Ikigai

Ikigai is your reason for being. It lies at the intersection of what you love, what you are good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

2. Embrace a Healthy Lifestyle

Maintain good health with moderation, physical activity, and a balanced diet. Japanese centenarians follow a diet rich in vegetables, fish, and minimal processed foods.

3. Live in the Moment

Focus on the present and practice mindfulness. Engaging in small, meaningful tasks can lead to a fulfilling life.

4. The Importance of Connection

Build and nurture strong social ties. Community and family bonds contribute significantly to happiness and longevity.

5. Stay Active

Always have a purpose. Keep your mind and body engaged through hobbies, learning, and activities that bring joy and purpose.

6. Flow: The Secret to Happiness

Achieve flow by immersing yourself in activities you enjoy and are skilled at. Flow occurs when you are completely absorbed in the task at hand.
